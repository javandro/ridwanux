About Me

Born and grew up in Banjarmasin, Indonesia. Since I was a child, I have loved art, which I have shown in my passion for drawing and play music. I took design faculty in college, design courses, and now working on and lived by designs.

I’ve been doing graphic design since 2012 as a freelancer and employees of 2 small companies. During this period i helped hundred SMB’s from across industry fields and non-profit organizations for their visual needs, from logo, company profile, stationary pack, and their marketing campaign.

I also have experience participating in many visual art exhibitions. Mostly my artwork is digital artwork, showing my calligraphic pieces.

I focused my career to digital product design and gain my experience in it since 2021. And from that time i have had an opportunity to built a B2B SaaS platform that serving F&B industries, used by thousands business operators in SEA.
